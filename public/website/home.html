Our Features

Enhanced chat
Customizable background

In this feature, the chat function has been optimised for the function of this meeting. There is a message whenever someone joins/leaves the meeting.

The background of the meeting can be changed, so that is allows a sense of customizability to the user and adds a visual appeal.

Clarity indicator
Screen sharing

Participants can indicate whenever they are confused (either about something specific, or in general) at any time.

This feature allows participants to share their screen in the meeting.

Active notes taking
Exam mode

Participants can take notes side-by-side in the meeting itself as it is going on; the notes can be saved as a word document/PDF at the end.

The exam mode allows for the participant's video feed to be recorded separately and then saved locally on their device.
